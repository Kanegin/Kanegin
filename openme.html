Will You Be My Valentine?

Dear Arriane,

Beneath the stars, where dreams take flight,
I weave these words, bathed in soft moonlight.
With each verse, a story unfolds,
A tale of emotions, silently told.

In the garden of hearts, a question blooms,
Asking, dear one, beyond these poetic rooms.
With courage borrowed from the night,
Will you be my valentine, in love's soft light?
